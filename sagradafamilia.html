Sagrada Família

The Sagrada Família, located in Barcelona, Spain, is a masterpiece of modernist architecture designed by Antoni Gaudí. Construction began in 1882 and continues to this day, making it one of the longest-running architectural projects in the world. This basilica is renowned for its stunning facades, intricate interiors, and spiritual symbolism.

Key Features

Height: 172 meters (564 feet) upon completion
Construction: Began in 1882, ongoing
Designer: Antoni Gaudí
Style: Modernist architecture
Materials: Stone, concrete, and glass

Visiting Information

Times: 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM (varies by season)
Prices: €26 for basic entry, €30 with guided audio tour
Tours: Available in multiple languages, including access to the towers
Special Offers: Discounts for students, children, and senior citizens

Dining Options

Nearby Cafés: Cozy spots serving traditional Catalan dishes
Takeaway Options: Perfect for enjoying meals in the park opposite the basilica
